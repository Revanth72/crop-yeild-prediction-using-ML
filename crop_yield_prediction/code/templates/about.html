DESCRIPTION

In general, agriculture plays an important role in the livelihood of people and country's economy.

Farmers usually have the mind-set of planting the same crop, using more fertilizers, and following the public choice. But now-a-days, food production and prediction is getting depleted due to unnatural climatic changes, which will adversely affect the economy of farmers by getting a poor yield and also help the farmers to remain less familiar in getting a high yield of crops.

Machine learning is one such advanced technique deployed to predict crop yield in agriculture.System predicts crop yield prediction from the gathering of past data.This system will be useful to predict the crop yield whether it fulfills the need of people or not in the future.
